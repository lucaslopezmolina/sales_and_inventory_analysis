## Business Questions

-Which products and categories drive most of the revenue?
-Which SKUs are underperforming in terms of margin?
-How does performance vary across regions and stores?
-Where should the business focus pricing, promotion, or inventory efforts?

## Analytical Approach

-Load raw sales data into a relational database (PostgreSQL)
-Normalize data into fact and dimension tables
-Use SQL to compute KPIs and performance metrics
-Build Power BI dashboards for business users
-Translate insights into commercial and operational recommendations

##Tools & Technologies

-PostgreSQL (SQL modeling & queries)
-DBeaver (database management)
-Power BI (dashboard & storytelling)
-Python (optional EDA)
